Tips

Get cleaner results

Use clear voice-over audio

Clean narration gives the render pipeline a better base and helps avoid awkward timing.

Choose focused images

Images with a clear subject work best with the zoom and transition effects used in the video.

Use specific keywords

Instead of broad words like nature, try focused phrases like mountain sunrise, hiking trail, forest lake.
